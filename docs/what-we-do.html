Life Academy

Founded in 2012 with the hopes of finding purpose in life, the Life & Basketball Academy will be the first elite student-athlete basketball academy in Liberia.

The academy will serve both high school boys and girls. Students will have the opportunity to graduate high school, attend college and secure a quality job upon completing our program. Some students may have the opportunity to leverage their athletic talents to secure scholarships at top high schools/colleges in the US and around the world.

The Academy will be located on a beautiful 12 acres in Charlesville, Monrovia, Liberia; just few miles from the Robert’s International Airport.

Interpersonal Skills Taught by Life & Basketball

Whether you’re playing competitively in a league or just for recreation at the park, basketball benefits your social life. You may form friendships that drift off at the close of the season or last for years. Even if you don’t become buddy-buddy with many members of your team, the skills you learn dealing with the different personality types can help you in situations off the court. You may learn to better relate to difficult personalities at work, for example. Basketball can give you more confidence in your daily life, and it can help you to accept those times you don’t win or get what you want

Camp for Young Adults

Each athlete will receive more advanced instruction with advanced moves/footwork. Designed for players who are serious about competing at the high school or even collegiate level in the future, the TLS Camp combines important life skills and high-level basketball instruction to a create a fun and intense training experience. Players will get the opportunity to learn skill-specific tips from Harris and fine-tune individual moves with the quality Life & Basketball camp coaches

Beginners

Younger kids (1st grade -5th) will receive more fundamental, age-appropriate instruction to lay a proper foundation of fundamentals for them to build on as they continue to grow and develop. Through positive reinforcement and motivation, campers learn basic basketball skills and concepts while they compete in fun contests and participate in dynamic drills. At the TLS Camp, young players are encouraged to work as a team as they are exposed to key skills and qualities that will help them improve and develop a love for basketball.

Life Nurtures

Life Nurtures will be an after school, youth development programs for the purposes of offering basketball clinics, academic tutoring and life skills lessons for youth. In so doing, Life Nurtures will work with local coaches, basketball clubs, and schools to create a program that aligns with the Life & Basketball Project mission.

All students receive weekly programming

Lady Life

Lady Life is women empowerment to help bridge the gender gap. At Life & Basketball Academy we believe that what men do women are capable of doing. The Lady Life program will encompass workshops to welder the gender gap of student-athlete goals.
